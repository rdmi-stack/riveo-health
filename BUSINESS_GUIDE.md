# 1. Executive Summary

## What Is Riveo Health?

Riveo Health is an **AI-native Healthcare Revenue & Interaction Operating System** that controls patient interactions, revenue cycles, and financial flow for healthcare organizations.

## The Problem

- Healthcare organizations lose **$125K+ per year** to preventable claim denials (MGMA)
- **18-25%** of claims are denied on first submission (AMA)
- Staff spend **30+ hours/week** on manual follow-ups (HBMA)
- Average **45-60 days** in accounts receivable (HFMA)
- Organizations use **10-15 disconnected systems** creating data silos (KLAS Research)
- **$262 billion** wasted annually on billing and insurance-related costs in the US (JAMA)

## The Solution

A single AI-native platform replacing 10+ disconnected tools:
- **Patient Interaction Agent** — AI chat, voice, SMS, email
- **Revenue Intelligence Engine** — Claims, denials, collections
- **AI Decision Engine** — Auto-coding, prediction, automation
- **Analytics & Insights** — Real-time dashboards, leakage detection
- **Universal Integrations** — 40+ EHR/PM connections

## The Opportunity

- Global Healthcare Spend: **$10T+**
- Admin & Billing Layer: **$2-3T**
- RCM Market: **$150-200B** (growing 12% CAGR)
- No AI-native company combining Interaction + Revenue + Financial Infrastructure

## Core Thesis

> Start as RCM → Grow into Platform → Become Healthcare Financial Infrastructure

# 3. Product Strategy

## 3.1 Product Principles

1. **AI-native, not AI-bolted** — Every feature is built with AI at the core
2. **One platform, not ten tools** — Replace the fragmented stack
3. **Results in days, not months** — 7-day implementation
4. **Self-improving** — Gets smarter with every claim processed
5. **Customer-obsessed** — Build what billing managers ask for, not what looks good in demos

## 3.2 Product Modules (Detailed)

### Module 1: Patient Interaction Agent
**Problem**: Front desk overwhelmed, patients waiting days for billing answers
**Solution**: AI agents across all channels

| Feature | Description | Tech |
|---------|-------------|------|
| AI Chat Agent | Real-time billing Q&A, eligibility checks, scheduling | LLM + RAG |
| Voice Agent | Call center automation, medical terminology NLU | Whisper + TTS |
| WhatsApp/SMS | Payment reminders, appointment confirmations | Twilio/WhatsApp Business |
| Email Automation | Statements, EOB explanations, follow-ups | Custom templates + AI |

**Key Metrics Target**: 70% auto-resolution, <3s response, 24/7 availability

### Module 2: Revenue Intelligence Engine
**Problem**: Claims team buried in spreadsheets, denials piling up
**Solution**: End-to-end claim lifecycle management

| Feature | Description | Tech |
|---------|-------------|------|
| Claim Scrubbing | Pre-submission validation against payer rules | Rules engine + ML |
| Denial Prevention | Predict which claims will be denied | XGBoost / Random Forest |
| Auto-Resubmission | Correct and resubmit denied claims | Workflow automation |
| Payment Follow-ups | Automated patient and payer follow-up sequences | Multi-channel outreach |

**Key Metrics Target**: 97% clean claim rate, 60% fewer denials, 18 days avg A/R

### Module 3: AI Decision Engine
**Problem**: Manual coding errors cause 30-40% of rejections
**Solution**: AI that codes, predicts, and recommends

| Feature | Description | Tech |
|---------|-------------|------|
| Auto-Coding | Clinical notes → ICD-10/CPT codes | Fine-tuned LLM |
| Denial Prediction | Rejection probability per claim | ML classification |
| Next Best Action | Appeal, resubmit, call payer, or escalate | Decision trees + ML |
| Conversational AI | Staff/patient query understanding | LLM + domain fine-tuning |

**Key Metrics Target**: 95% coding accuracy, <2s per claim, 40% less manual review

### Module 4: Analytics & Insights
**Problem**: Revenue leakage discovered too late (quarterly reviews)
**Solution**: Real-time visibility into everything

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Revenue Dashboards | Live claims pipeline, payment status, denial trends |
| Leakage Detection | Missed charges, under-coding, unbilled procedures |
| Payer Performance | Which payers deny most, pay slowest, underperform |
| Staff Productivity | Biller/coder performance, bottleneck identification |

**Key Metrics Target**: Real-time data, 50+ reports, $78K avg leakage found per audit

### Module 5: Universal Integrations
**Problem**: 10-15 disconnected systems creating data silos
**Solution**: Plug into everything, replace nothing

| Integration | Systems |
|-------------|---------|
| EHR/PM | Epic, Cerner, athenahealth, eClinicalWorks, 40+ more |
| Clearinghouses | Availity, Change Healthcare, Waystar, Trizetto |
| Payer APIs | Direct connections for eligibility, claim status, ERA |
| Payment | Stripe, Square, credit card, ACH, payment plans |
| Standards | HL7 FHIR R4, X12 EDI, REST APIs, webhooks |

**Key Metrics Target**: 40+ integrations, <7 days setup, 99.9% uptime

# 7. Sales Playbook

## 7.1 Sales Process

```
Lead → Qualify → Audit → Demo → Proposal → Close → Onboard
```

### Step 1: Lead Generation (Day 1)
- Inbound: Website form, content downloads, audit requests
- Outbound: Cold email sequence (5 touches over 14 days)
- Referral: Warm introductions from existing clients/partners

### Step 2: Qualification Call (Day 1-3)
**Duration**: 15 minutes
**Goal**: Determine fit and urgency

**Qualifying Questions**:
1. How many providers/claims per month?
2. What's your current denial rate?
3. How many FTEs on your billing team?
4. What systems do you use (EHR, PM, clearinghouse)?
5. What's your biggest billing frustration right now?
6. Have you evaluated other solutions recently?

**Disqualify if**:
- Less than 100 claims/month (too small for ROI)
- Already locked in a 3-year outsourced RCM contract
- No budget authority in the conversation

### Step 3: Free Revenue Audit (Day 3-7)
**Duration**: 48 hours to deliver results
**Goal**: Show them exactly how much they're losing

**What We Analyze**:
- Denial rate by payer and reason code
- Average days in A/R
- Top denial reasons (preventable vs non-preventable)
- Estimated revenue leakage from coding gaps
- Staff time allocation analysis

**Deliverable**: 1-page audit report showing:
- Current state (their numbers)
- Industry benchmarks
- Estimated annual recovery with Riveo Health
- ROI calculation

**This is the most important step.** When a CFO sees they're losing $200K/year and you can recover 60% of it, the sale practically closes itself.

### Step 4: Live Demo (Day 7-10)
**Duration**: 30 minutes
**Goal**: Show the product solving THEIR specific problems

**Demo Flow**:
1. (2 min) Recap their audit findings
2. (8 min) Show claim scrubbing on a claim like theirs
3. (5 min) Show denial prediction on their top denial reasons
4. (5 min) Show analytics dashboard with their metrics
5. (5 min) Show patient AI agent handling a billing query
6. (5 min) Q&A and next steps

**Rules**:
- Never give a generic demo. Always customize to their specialty/payers
- Always have their audit numbers visible during the demo
- End with: "Based on your audit, you're losing $X/year. We can recover 40-60% of that. Should I send a proposal?"

### Step 5: Proposal (Day 10-12)
**One-pager** including:
- Their problem (from audit)
- Recommended tier
- Monthly price
- Estimated ROI (conservative)
- Implementation timeline (7 days)
- No-risk terms (month-to-month, cancel anytime)

### Step 6: Close (Day 12-21)
**Objection Handling**:

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Too expensive" | "You're losing $X/year to denials. Our annual cost is $Y. That's Z:1 ROI." |
| "We're locked in a contract" | "We integrate alongside your current tools. Start with claim scrubbing only." |
| "Our staff can't handle another tool" | "Implementation takes 7 days. We reduce staff workload by 40%, not add to it." |
| "We need to think about it" | "What specifically would help you decide? Can we do a 30-day pilot?" |
| "We need board approval" | "I'll prepare an ROI summary for your board. What metrics matter most to them?" |

### Step 7: Onboard (Day 1-7 post-close)
- Day 1: Integration setup (EHR/PM connection)
- Day 2-3: Data migration and configuration
- Day 4-5: Team training (1 hour session)
- Day 6: Go-live with monitoring
- Day 7: First week review call

## 10.4 AI Model Development

### Denial Prediction Model
- **Training Data**: 50M+ historical claims (public datasets + synthetic)
- **Features**: CPT codes, ICD codes, payer, modifiers, provider, timing, prior denials
- **Model**: Gradient Boosted Trees (XGBoost) + Neural Network ensemble
- **Target Accuracy**: 95%+ (current: testing at 93%)
- **Retraining**: Weekly with new claim outcomes

### Auto-Coding Model
- **Architecture**: Fine-tuned LLM on medical coding datasets
- **Input**: Clinical notes (free text)
- **Output**: ICD-10, CPT codes with confidence scores
- **Target Accuracy**: 95% (current: testing at 92%)
- **Validation**: Human-in-the-loop for low-confidence codes

### Conversational AI
- **Architecture**: RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation)
- **Knowledge Base**: Payer policies, billing rules, patient FAQs
- **Languages**: English (Phase 1), Spanish (Phase 2)
- **Channels**: Web chat, voice, WhatsApp, SMS

- Year 1 ARPA: $2,500
- Year 2 ARPA: $3,500 (tier upgrade + usage)
