# Riveo Health — Marketing & Sales Playbook (80/20)

> **80/20 Rule**: 80% of your early clients will come from 20% of your efforts.
> This document focuses only on what actually drives revenue. No fluff.

---

## The Truth About Healthcare Sales

Every successful healthcare SaaS company — athenahealth, Waystar, Practo, Kareo — used **offline field sales** for their first 100-500 clients before layering on digital. Jonathan Bush (athenahealth) personally drove to OB/GYN practices. Practo sent 2,000+ reps door-to-door in Bangalore. This is not optional — it's the playbook.

## Channel 1: Offline Field Sales Team (PRIMARY — 60% of Revenue)

This is your #1 acquisition channel. Everything else supports this.

### Why Offline First

| Digital sales | Field sales |
|--------------|-------------|
| 2-5% cold email response rate | 10-20% in-person meeting rate |
| Prospect reads your email for 3 seconds | You get 15-30 minutes face-to-face |
| Can't show real ROI | Walk in with their revenue leakage number |
| Trust takes months to build | Trust starts in the first handshake |
| Easy to ignore | Impossible to ignore when you're standing there |

### Who to Hire

**Hire for rolodex, not resume.**

The ideal field rep:
- Worked at a hospital, billing company, or healthcare IT vendor before
- Already knows 20+ billing managers or practice administrators personally
- Understands claim lifecycle, denial codes, EHR systems
- Comfortable walking into a hospital unannounced
- Doesn't need to be taught healthcare — needs to be taught your product

**Where to find them:**
- Former medical billing staff who want better pay
- Ex-sales reps from Practo, Tally (India), athenahealth, or RCM companies
- Hospital administration graduates with connections
- LinkedIn search: "Revenue Cycle" + "Sales" + [your target city]

### Daily Routine for Field Reps

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 8:00-8:30 AM | Pre-call planning — review CRM, research today's targets (hospital size, recent news, leadership changes) |
| 8:30-9:00 AM | Morning meeting with team — share wins, blockers, plan routes |
| 9:00 AM-12:00 PM | **In-person visits** — 3-5 hospital/practice drop-ins or scheduled meetings. Morning is best (billing teams less slammed) |
| 12:00-1:00 PM | **Lunch-and-learn** at a prospect office (bring food — budget Rs 500-1000 / $30-50 per lunch) |
| 1:00-4:00 PM | More visits + scheduled demos. Afternoons harder for cold drop-ins |
| 4:00-5:30 PM | CRM updates, follow-up emails/WhatsApp messages, schedule next day's appointments |
| 5:30-6:00 PM | Daily debrief with team — what worked, what didn't |

**Target: 4-5 visits/day, 20-25 visits/week per rep.**

### How to Get Past the Gatekeeper

1. **Call ahead**: "I'd like to schedule 10 minutes with [Billing Manager name] to discuss the revenue leakage findings we've prepared for [Hospital Name]." Having something specific and personalized gets through.

2. **Befriend the front desk**: Learn their name. Bring chai/coffee. They control access and influence decisions.

3. **Go early or late**: Decision-makers are more accessible before 9 AM or after 5 PM when the gatekeeper has left.

4. **The referral**: "Dr. [Name] suggested I speak with your billing team about the denial issue he mentioned." A personal introduction converts 3-5x higher.

5. **WhatsApp first (India)**: Many Indian hospital administrators are reachable via WhatsApp. Send a short voice note + one-page PDF before visiting in person.

6. **Vendor days**: Many larger Indian hospitals have designated days/times for vendor meetings. Ask the front desk when these are.

7. **FedEx trick (US)**: Mail a FedEx package (not regular mail — FedEx gets opened) with a personalized leakage estimate. Follow up in person 3-5 days later.

### What Field Reps Carry

**Physical (non-negotiable):**
- iPad/laptop with **offline demo** (hospital WiFi is unreliable or restricted)
- **One-page leave-behind** (laminated) — problem stat, what Riveo does, 3 proof points, QR code to book demo
- **ROI calculator printout** customized to that hospital's estimated size
- **Compliance packet** — HIPAA BAA template, SOC 2 cert, security overview
- Business cards
- **3-minute demo video** (backup for when you only get a short window)

**Digital (on phone/tablet):**
- Free Revenue Audit Tool (riveohealth.com/audit) — show them live
- WhatsApp-ready content: short demo video, one-page PDF, pricing overview
- CRM app (HubSpot mobile) for instant note-taking

**Never bring:**
- Long slide decks (nobody reads them)
- Generic brochures (they go in the trash)
- Anything not customized to their hospital

### The Visit Script (What to Actually Say)

**Step 1: The opener (30 seconds)**
> "Hi, I'm [Name] from Riveo Health. We help [specialty] practices recover revenue that's being lost to claim denials and billing errors. We typically find $78K-$500K in annual leakage that practices don't know about. I have a quick analysis for [Hospital Name] — is [Billing Manager] available for 5 minutes?"

**Step 2: If you get the meeting (5-15 minutes)**
> "Before I show you anything about our product, can I ask — what's your current denial rate? How many claims does your team process per month? What's your biggest billing headache right now?"

**Listen. Take notes. Don't pitch yet.**

Then:
> "Based on what you've told me, a practice your size with a [X]% denial rate is likely losing [$amount] per year. I can run a detailed analysis for free — takes 2 minutes. Want to see it?"

**Step 3: Show the audit tool on your tablet**
- Open riveohealth.com/audit
- Enter their numbers (Quick Assessment mode)
- Show them the leakage report in real-time
- Let the numbers do the selling

**Step 4: The close**
> "This analysis shows [$X] in recoverable revenue. We can start fixing this in 7 days. No contract, month-to-month. If we don't deliver ROI in 30 days, you cancel. Can I set up a deeper demo with your team this week?"

**Step 5: If they say no or "send me info"**
> "Absolutely. I'll send the full report to your email right now. But — the numbers don't change. You're losing $[X] every month this stays unfixed. Can I check back in 2 weeks?"

Leave the one-page sheet. Send WhatsApp/email follow-up within 2 hours.

### The Sales Funnel (Realistic Conversion Rates)

| Stage | Cold Visit | Warm/Referral |
|-------|:---:|:---:|
| Visit → Got a meeting | 10-20% | 40-60% |
| Meeting → Qualified opportunity | 25-40% | 50-70% |
| Qualified → Demo delivered | 60-80% | 80-90% |
| Demo → Pilot (free 30 days) | 20-40% | 40-60% |
| Pilot → Paid client | 50-70% | 70-85% |
| **End-to-end: Visit → Paid** | **2-6%** | **10-20%** |

**What this means:**
- 2 reps × 20 visits/week = 40 visits/week
- At 4% cold conversion = **1-2 new clients/week**
- At 15% warm conversion = **5-6 new clients/week** (once pipeline is warm)
- **Month 1 realistic**: 3-5 signed clients
- **Month 3 realistic**: 15-25 total clients

## Channel 2: Referral Program (25% of Revenue)

The second-highest ROI channel. Every signed client should bring 2-3 more.

### Why Referrals Dominate

- Billing managers know other billing managers
- Doctors refer doctors
- Practice administrators attend the same local meetings
- Referral conversion is **3-5x higher** than cold outreach
- Cost of acquisition is near zero

### Referral Program Structure

| Tier | Reward | For |
|------|--------|-----|
| **Client referral** | 1 month free (or Rs 10K / $500 cash) | Every signed referral |
| **Consultant referral** | 15-20% recurring revenue share | Healthcare consultants, billing experts who recommend Riveo |
| **Doctor-to-doctor** | Rs 5K / $250 gift card | Doctor refers another practice |

### How to Ask for Referrals

**Timing**: Ask at the "success moment" — when the first audit shows results, when the first month's recovered revenue is visible, when they say "this is great."

**The script:**
> "Glad it's working. Who else in your network is dealing with the same denial issues? I'd love to help them too — and you get a free month for every practice you introduce us to."

### Referral Tracking

- Track in CRM (HubSpot custom field)
- Send the referral reward within 48 hours of the new client signing (speed builds trust)
- Send a thank-you note (handwritten in India — this stands out massively)

## Channel 3: Strategic Partnerships (10% of Revenue)

### Partner Types

| Partner | Why They Refer You | What They Get |
|---------|-------------------|---------------|
| **RCM consultants** | They advise hospitals on billing — you're a tool they can recommend | 15-20% revenue share or white-label access |
| **CA firms / accounting firms** (India) | They handle hospital finances, see billing mess firsthand | Per-client referral fee (Rs 10K-25K) |
| **EHR/PMS vendors** | Their clients ask for better billing tools | Integration partnership + co-marketing |
| **Hospital management consultants** | They restructure hospital operations | Revenue share on referred clients |
| **Medical billing training institutes** | They train billers who then work at hospitals | Offer students free access → they become advocates |

### How to Build Partnerships

1. **Identify 10 RCM consultants / CA firms** in your target cities
2. **Offer them free access** to the platform for their own use
3. **Create a partner portal** (even a simple Google Sheet to start) where they can track referrals
4. **Do a joint webinar** or case study once you have results
5. **Pay fast** — referral commissions within 48 hours

## Channel 5: Digital Marketing (Support Channel — Not Primary)

Digital doesn't replace field sales. It **warms up** prospects before the rep visits and **nurtures** them after.

### Website (Already Built)

- riveohealth.com — 29 pages, SEO-optimized, demo form, audit tool
- **Key pages for field sales support**: /audit (show during visits), /demo (prospects book after visits)

### LinkedIn (15 min/day)

**Founder's personal LinkedIn is the most important marketing asset.**

| Activity | Frequency | Purpose |
|----------|-----------|---------|
| Post industry pain point stats | 3x/week | Build authority, get inbound |
| Comment on hospital admin posts | Daily (5 min) | Build relationships before cold outreach |
| Share audit tool results (anonymized) | 1x/week | Prove the product works |
| Connect with billing managers | 10/day | Build prospect list |

**Content that works:**
- "Did you know 60% of denied claims are never reworked? That's cash in the trash." + link to audit
- "We analyzed 50K claims last month. The #1 denial reason was..." + insight
- Short video (60 sec) showing the audit tool in action

### WhatsApp Business (Critical for India)

- Set up WhatsApp Business account
- Create a **broadcast list** of prospects
- Send weekly value content: 1 industry stat + 1 Riveo insight + audit tool link
- Respond to inquiries within 30 minutes (this is expected in India)

### Google Ads (Start Later — After 20+ Clients)

- Target: "medical billing software", "reduce claim denials", "RCM automation"
- Budget: $2K-$5K/month (US) or Rs 50K-1L/month (India)
- Drive to /audit page — the free tool is the conversion mechanism
- **Don't start ads until your field sales process is proven** — ads amplify what works, they don't create product-market fit

### Email Marketing (Nurture Only)

- Collect emails from: audit tool users, demo requests, event contacts, field visit follow-ups
- Send 1 email/week: industry insight + CTA to book demo
- Tool: HubSpot free (already have for CRM)
- **Don't buy email lists** — healthcare contacts are sensitive, bought lists have terrible deliverability

### SEO / Blog (Long Game)

- 6 blog posts already exist on the site
- Add 2 posts/month targeting: "how to reduce claim denials", "medical billing AI", "revenue cycle automation"
- This is a 6-12 month investment — won't drive revenue in month 1-3

## Pricing for Field Sales

| Tier | Monthly Price | Target | What Rep Says |
|------|:---:|--------|---------------|
| **Starter** | Rs 50K-1L / $1,500-$2K | 1-5 doctors | "Less than the cost of one part-time biller" |
| **Growth** | Rs 1L-2L / $3K-$5K | 5-25 doctors | "We save you 3-5x what we cost" |
| **Enterprise** | Rs 2L-5L / $5K-$10K | 25+ doctors | "Custom ROI analysis for your org" |

**Field sales pricing rules:**
1. Never discount more than 20% on first visit — you lose negotiation room
2. Always offer **free 30-day pilot** instead of a discount
3. Show the ROI math: "We cost Rs 1L/month, we recover Rs 5L/month. That's 5x return"
4. Month-to-month always — this is your #1 objection killer in the field

| They Say | You Say |
|----------|---------|
| "We already have billing software" | "We don't replace it — we make it smarter. We catch what it misses." |

## The 3 Rules That Matter

### Rule 1: The Founder Sells First

Don't hire reps until YOU have closed 3-5 clients personally. You need to:
- Know the objections firsthand
- Understand what demo flow works
- Feel the sales cycle in your bones
- Build the script your reps will use

### Rule 2: Free Audit is the Weapon

Every visit, every call, every WhatsApp message should lead to:
> "Let me run a free revenue audit for you. 2 minutes. No commitment. I'll show you exactly what you're losing."

The audit tool you built at /audit is your sharpest weapon. Use it in every conversation.

### Rule 3: Referrals Over Everything

One happy client who refers 3 others is worth more than 100 cold visits. Build the referral mechanism from day 1:
- Ask for referrals at the success moment
- Pay referral rewards within 48 hours
- Make it embarrassingly easy to refer (WhatsApp forward of your one-pager)
